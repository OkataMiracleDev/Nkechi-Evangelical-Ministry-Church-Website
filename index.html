Meet Our Prophetess

Prophetess Favour Nkechi Okata

The love of God for mankind, makes him raise one in every generation, whom He sends to bring about transformation in the lives of men, and one of such person is Prophetess (Mrs) Nkechi Favour Okata. Prophetess (Mrs) Nkechi Favour Okata is a spiritual counsellor, anointed and commissioned to preach Salvation, Faith, Healing, Breaking of yokes, Deliverance, and Restoration.
